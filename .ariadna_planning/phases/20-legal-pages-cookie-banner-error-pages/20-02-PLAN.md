<objective>
Upgrade the cookie notice dismissal from session-scoped to persistent. Currently, `CookieNoticesController#dismiss` writes to `session[:cookie_notice_shown]` which is cleared on logout. The phase 20 locked decision requires upgrading to `cookies[:cookie_notice_dismissed]` expiring 365 days from now, so the notice never reappears after first dismissal.

Purpose: Session-only dismissal means the notice reappears on every new session — a PECR compliance nuisance. A persistent cookie matches user expectations for a "dismiss once" banner on a strictly-necessary-cookies-only site.
Output: Updated `CookieNoticesController#dismiss` action; updated render condition in application layout.
</objective>
