The content must cover these sections at minimum:

1. **Who we are** — Asthma Buddy (data controller). Contact: privacy@asthmabuddy.app. Registered in England and Wales.
2. **What personal data we collect** — Email address (account registration); health data you enter (symptom logs, peak flow readings, medication records, dose logs, health events, personal best values); server logs (IP address, request timestamps) retained for 90 days for security purposes.
3. **Why we process your data (lawful basis)** — Performance of contract (providing the service you signed up for) for account data; explicit consent (UK GDPR Art. 9(2)(a)) for special category health data; legitimate interests (security and fraud prevention) for server logs.
4. **Special category health data** — Your health logs are special category data under UK GDPR. We process them solely to provide the service to you. We do not share, sell, or use health data for advertising or profiling.
5. **How we store your data** — Data is stored in an encrypted database on a server located in the UK/EEA. Passwords are hashed using bcrypt and never stored in plaintext. We do not use third-party analytics or advertising services.
6. **Data retention** — Account data and health logs are retained until you delete your account. Server logs are retained for 90 days. Upon account deletion, all personal data is permanently erased within 30 days.
7. **Your rights under UK GDPR** — Right to access; right to rectification; right to erasure (delete your account from Settings at any time); right to restriction of processing; right to data portability (contact us); right to object; right to withdraw consent.
8. **Cookies** — We use only a session cookie, which is essential for the service to function. No tracking or advertising cookies are used.
9. **Changes to this policy** — We will notify you by email of material changes before they take effect.
10. **Contact and complaints** — Contact us at privacy@asthmabuddy.app. You have the right to lodge a complaint with the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) at ico.org.uk if you believe we have not handled your data correctly.
